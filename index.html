Welcome to the Hydroponic Controller project.

The code is currently in early development phase and it being changed on a regular basis.

The goal of the project is to create an open source controller for people who grow food the hydroponics way.

The SW currently does the following:

Monitors the pH and EC levels of the water, Monitors the temperature of the water and air, Controls a water pump, heater and lights via wireless plugs Informs the user if the water temperature, pH, EC or air temperature go out of preset ranges. Lets the user program when the water pump will pump.
